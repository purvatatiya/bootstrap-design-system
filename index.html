My Design System

by Purva Tatiya

Lume Design System is a sunset-inspired design framework that draws inspiration from the rich colors and serene transitions of a sunset. This system brings warmth and tranquility to user interfaces, blending vibrant hues and subtle gradients to create a visually engaging and harmonious experience. With a focus on simplicity and elegance, Lume aims to enhance both functionality and aesthetic appeal, offering a versatile foundation for building intuitive and immersive digital products.
